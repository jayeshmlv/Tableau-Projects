# HR Analytics Dashboard | Tableau

## Overview

An interactive Tableau dashboard designed to analyze workforce demographics, employee attrition, job satisfaction, and overall HR performance, helping organizations make data-driven workforce decisions.

## Key Features

- Employee Demographics Analysis
- Attrition Tracking & Insights
- Department-wise Workforce Analysis
- Job Satisfaction Monitoring
- HR KPI Tracking
- Interactive Filters

## Key Insights

- The organization has **7,984 active employees**, with **8,950 total hires** and **966 terminations**, indicating overall workforce growth.
- **Operations** is the largest department, followed by **Sales** and **Customer Service**, reflecting the organization's operational focus.
- Around **70% of employees are based at the headquarters**, while **30% work from branch locations**, suggesting a centralized workforce structure.
- The workforce demonstrates a relatively balanced gender distribution (**54% Male, 46% Female**), indicating good gender diversity.
- Employees with **Master's and PhD qualifications** have higher average salaries compared to those with Bachelor's and High School education, reflecting the value placed on advanced qualifications.
- Salary generally increases with employee age, suggesting compensation grows with experience and career progression.
- Employees with higher educational qualifications appear to have a greater representation in **Excellent** performance ratings, indicating a possible relationship between education and performance.

---

## Business Recommendations

- Continue monitoring hiring and termination trends to support effective workforce planning and resource allocation.
- Regularly review workforce distribution across departments and locations to ensure staffing aligns with business requirements.
- Maintain and strengthen diversity initiatives by tracking workforce representation across departments and leadership roles.
- Invest in employee learning and development programs to enhance skills and improve overall performance.
- Conduct periodic compensation reviews to ensure salary progression remains aligned with employee qualifications, experience, and market standards.
- Perform deeper workforce analysis, such as department-wise attrition rates and tenure analysis, to support more informed HR decision-making.
